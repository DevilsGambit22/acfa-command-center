Welcome to And Chess For All Official (ACFA), an active and welcoming Chess.com community for players of every rating. Compete, improve, make friends, and enjoy the game with people who believe everyone deserves a place at the board.

ACFA brings members together through Daily Team Matches, Vote Chess, arenas, Swiss tournaments, Chess960, live chess streams, club discussions, and community projects. Participation is always optional, and every member is encouraged to join in at their own pace.

Whether you are completely new to chess, returning to the game, or an experienced competitor, ACFA offers friendship, competition, collaboration, and a growing network built around a shared love of chess.
